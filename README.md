A clinic management ER diagram includes entities such as Patient, Doctor, Appointment, Consultation, Test, Report, and Payment. A Patient books an Appointment with a Doctor, and each appointment leads to a Consultation. During the consultation, the Doctor may prescribe Tests, which later generate Reports. Payments are associated with either the Appointment or the Consultation, completing the workflow from scheduling to diagnosis and billing.
